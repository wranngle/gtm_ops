What is Modeled Opportunity?

Modeled opportunity represents potential revenue gains from improved efficiency, faster response times, and reduced errors. Unlike hard savings (which are guaranteed labor cost reductions), modeled opportunity captures the upside value that automation enables but cannot guarantee. This includes faster lead response, reduced customer churn, and increased throughput capacity.
